About the Awards

A Tradition of Recognition

Named in honor of Evan Dickerson, whose passion for bluegrass music inspired generations of listeners and musicians alike, these awards celebrate the artistry, dedication, and spirit of the bluegrass community.

Each year, fans and peers come together to recognize outstanding achievement across the genre — from breakthrough new voices to lifetime contributions that keep the tradition alive.

Community-Driven

Winners are chosen by the people who know and love bluegrass best. Your vote shapes who receives recognition each year.

Built to Endure

Categories and honorees may change with each season, but the mission remains the same: to honor excellence with dignity and permanence.

Award Categories

Recognizing the Finest

Nominees are evaluated across a range of categories that reflect the breadth and depth of bluegrass music. Cast your vote in each category that matters to you.

Artist of the Year

Honoring an individual whose artistry and impact defined the year in bluegrass.

Album of the Year

Celebrating a full-length release that stands as a landmark achievement.

Song of the Year

Recognizing a single track that captured hearts and moved the genre forward.

Band of the Year

Awarded to the ensemble whose collective performance set the standard.

New Artist of the Year

Spotlighting a rising voice making an extraordinary early impression.

Instrumentalist of the Year

Honoring virtuosity and musical voice on banjo, fiddle, mandolin, and beyond.
