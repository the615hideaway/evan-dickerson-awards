About the Awards

A Tradition of Recognition

Named in honor of Evan Dickerson, whose passion for bluegrass music inspired generations of listeners and musicians alike, these awards celebrate the artistry, dedication, and spirit of the bluegrass community.

Each year, fans and peers come together to recognize outstanding achievement across the genre — from breakthrough new voices to lifetime contributions that keep the tradition alive.

Community-Driven

Winners are chosen by the people who know and love bluegrass best. Your vote shapes who receives recognition each year.

Built to Endure

Categories and honorees may change with each season, but the mission remains the same: to honor excellence with dignity and permanence.
